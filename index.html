    The Integrated Design & Media Program (IDM) is a place that fosters creative practice, design research and multidisciplinary experimentation with emerging media technologies.

            IDM is a ‘STEAM‘ program combining artistic inquiry with scientific research and technological practice to explore the social, cultural and ethical potentials of emerging technologies. We focus on how to create new experiences with technologies but also on what is worthmaking and why.
